Focus · Ways to partner & collaborate 07 / Ways to work together

More than one
way in.

This isn't a rate card. It's a set of things we can mix and share, sometimes commercially, sometimes as a straight value-for-value swap, and often just to build something with people we want to know better.

01
Contra & barter

Trade reach, not invoices.

Exchange ecosystem exposure for products, services, data, venues or access that help us serve the community better. You promote ours, we promote yours, and no money need change hands.

02
Content collaboration

Make something worth watching.

Co-created video, podcast episodes, Spotlight features, promoted articles and research. Your expertise, our audience, exactly how our top videos already work.

03
Relationships

Get into the right rooms.

Introductions, event hosting, retreats, advisory and community access. The slow, compounding kind of value that outlasts any single campaign.

04
Sponsorship & sales

When it's simply commercial.

When a straightforward commercial fit makes sense, the usual routes are open too: web, video, social, newsletters and events. One conversation, many options.
