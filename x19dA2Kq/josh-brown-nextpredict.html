The market context for the session

Prediction markets are no longer a niche betting or crypto conversation. They are being pulled into public-market analysis, broker strategy, listed operator strategy, media distribution, risk controls and regulatory debate.

RobinhoodHas publicly described prediction markets as its fastest-growing product line by revenue.
Retail flowEvent contracts are now part of the same attention economy as options, crypto, sports and financial media.
Market structureThe serious money question is about distribution, liquidity, market making, data, clearing, compliance and trust.

This gives the session a cleaner angle than a generic discussion about whether prediction markets are interesting. The better question is whether the business model survives once the first wave of attention, sports volume and novelty pricing settles down.
