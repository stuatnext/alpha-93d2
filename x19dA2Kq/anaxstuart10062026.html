01 · Overview

What this is and what it is not

This page makes the scope explicit and keeps the current planning process as the base layer.

This is

A consolidation view

It combines the current product planning process with the Commercial layer needed for Sales, Marketing and CRM readiness.

This is

A decision-gate map

It shows which decisions need to happen before GTM, Sales, Marketing, CRM setup and collateral production scale.

This is

A recovery plan for 2026

Because today is 5 June, the current cycle is already compressed. The goal is to align on a realistic route to 31 July.

What this does not do

Does not replace September Drive

September remains the alignment and announcement moment.

Does not move Ops ownership

Project Owners still own project coordination, dependencies and operational delivery.

Does not tell Media how to deliver

Media keeps ownership of editorial boundaries, production feasibility and product delivery.

Does not force premature assets

Final collateral only follows approved product, pricing, package and delivery decisions.

04 · Future rhythm

How this should work from 2027 onward

The future version should create enough runway for proper strategy, budget-cycle conversations, functional planning and delivery readiness.

After each event or cycle

Evidence within 2 to 4 weeks

What sold, what resisted, what delivered, what underperformed, what buyers asked for and what data is missing.

Before sales scales

Commercial foundation approved

Products, pricing, packages, targets, margin logic, discount rules and delivery boundaries approved before broad outreach.

Before campaigns scale

GTM and CRM agreed

GTM, Sales, Marketing and CRM strategies built from the same approved commercial foundation.
