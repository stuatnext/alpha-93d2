01 · Mission and positioning

The strategic decision is made

NEXTPredict is the institutional meeting point for prediction markets. The team should stop debating the category and start translating the position into pages, campaigns, sales conversations, content and delivery.

Why now

The category is forming in public

Prediction markets and event contracts are moving from niche conversation to institutional, regulatory, operator, product and capital-market relevance. The window to define the serious version of the category is open now.

Why NEXT.io

We have unfair infrastructure

NEXT.io already has event delivery, media capability, senior relationships, commercial experience and the scars from complex launches.

Positioning

Wall Street first

Lead with finance, trading, event contracts, compliance and infrastructure. Operators matter through product learning, partnership access and category intelligence.

What it is not

Not a betting conference

Not an iGaming expo, crypto hype event, retail trading show, legal seminar, startup demo day or sportsbook conference.

Mission line: We are not promoting a conference. We are building the institutional meeting point for a category before others dilute it.

Value proposition

NEXTPredict solves a market-coordination problem. The people shaping prediction markets sit across finance, regulation, operators, exchanges, data, legal, media and capital.

Attendees

Market clarity

One concentrated room to understand the market, meet decision-makers and benchmark thinking.

Sponsors

Category ownership

Visible position, buyer access, meeting opportunities and proof assets before the market gets crowded.

Operators

Strategic learning

Product, compliance, partnership and commercial intelligence without gambling-adjacent hype.

Investors

Early signal

Access to founders, exchanges, data providers and infrastructure companies before consensus forms.

Vocabulary rules

Language protects the category. It also protects the room quality and sponsor story.

UseAvoidOwners enforcing
Prediction markets, event contracts, information markets, market integrity, compliance architecture, liquidity, infrastructure, institutional summit.Betting markets, gambling disruption, bet on anything, crypto casino, regulatory loophole, sportsbook conference, iGaming expo.HPL, Media, Rory, Stuart, Will and Oli.
Banned creative drift: no casino language, no “bet on everything,” no gambling-disruption framing, no unapproved DraftKings or Kalshi proximity, no public claims without support.
