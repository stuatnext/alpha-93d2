02 Where marketing sits

Marketing is the growth engine inside Commercial.

Marketing reports into the Commercial Department alongside Sales and Revenue Operations, so the whole revenue engine is accountable as one system. Your job is to make marketing a strategic engine, not a service desk.

Pillar · Marketing

Influence & demand

Brand authority, demand generation, qualified audience growth, and marketing-sourced & marketing-influenced pipeline. This is yours.

Pillar · Sales

Revenue closure

Pipeline progression, forecast accuracy, margin discipline, expansion and retention. Led by Will.

Pillar · RevOps

The infrastructure

CRM architecture, lifecycle stages, attribution, dashboards and data hygiene. RevOps owns the system; marketing uses it well.

❯

The principle that runs through everything: influence is treated as a measurable commercial asset — it lowers acquisition cost, strengthens retention and pricing power, and compounds over time. Revenue growth that isn't backed by strengthening influence is considered fragile. Your role is leadership, strategy and systems — owning the strategy across events, media and brand — not hands-on campaign execution.
