Why ICE should care

ICE already sits on the most valuable question in the category.

From probability feed to institutional product

ICE has already moved prediction-market data into a professional market-data context through Polymarket Signals and Sentiment, normalising and structuring Polymarket data for institutional investors.

The event now needs to show that the buyer universe is real: data clients, trading desks, exchanges, brokers, market makers, research teams, compliance leaders and operators.

The commercial point for ICE is simple: NEXTPredict concentrates the people who can turn prediction-market probabilities into workflow, product and revenue.

Reference point: ICE Polymarket Signals and Sentiment announcement, February 2026.

Decision criteria for ICE

  • The agenda is credible enough for senior ICE involvement.
  • The audience contains serious financial-services and data-product buyers.
  • The speaker mix moves beyond consumer hype into market structure, data, integrity and institutional workflow.
  • ICE can participate without looking like it is simply endorsing the loudest retail part of the category.
