Tier 1 and Tier 2 pricing should be confirmed using traffic, commercial demand and category value. 5. Product assumptions behind the target

News: EUR 1.96M

  • Megabanner and Skyscraper need bridge products first because they are the proven sellers.
  • Sticky banner and category sponsorships are promising, but not yet proven products.
  • Category sponsorships now use Tier 1 and Tier 2 pricing because the 10 news categories do not carry equal traffic or commercial value.
  • Legacy banner renewals are deliberately not mapped as a product because that would confuse the sales team and clients.
  • Newsletter, PR placements and promoted content stay inside News.

Podcast: EUR 300k

  • Pragmatic Play and Pragmatic Solutions renewal should be modelled at EUR 110k with added distribution.
  • Paid guests move out of Podcast and into Spotlight.
  • NEXTPredict Podcast and Conor's show should not carry heavy revenue until launch, cadence, ownership and sponsor decks are confirmed.
  • No podcast product should include paid episode access or paid guest access.
  • Pierre's capacity is a constraint. Do not build the number on wishful output.

Spotlight: EUR 300k

  • Spotlight absorbs paid guest demand from Podcast.
  • Annual series should be sold as a year-long story package, not one-off video inventory.
  • Pragmatic Solutions gives proof that higher-volume series are deliverable, with 24 episodes sold.
  • Kim needs to confirm delivery capacity before this target is final.

Research Reports: EUR 200k

  • Research gets its own target in 2027.
  • 2025 had EUR 81k bundled into News.
  • 2026 has EUR 118.2k achieved already.
  • Target depends on production capacity and clear pricing for reports, white papers and data-led articles.
