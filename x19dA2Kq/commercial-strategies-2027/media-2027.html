5 · What changed from the last draft (and why)

  • Podcast €300k → €200k. The product is shrinking (every-other-week, no paid guests) and missed 2026 (72%). Modelling it up wasn't defensible. New shows (Robin's €100k, Connor's) are upside.
  • Spotlight stays the growth engine for multimedia (€300k) because it absorbs the paid-guest demand leaving Podcast.
  • News legacy banner bridge raised to €300k. Megabanner/Skyscraper are sold out and proven — we keep selling them through the transition rather than switching them off.
  • New verticals moved fully to upside. The €2.60M is the like-for-like portfolio only.
  • Base corrected to the 18 Jun tracker (News achieved €1,475,909; media-in-scope €1.99M).
