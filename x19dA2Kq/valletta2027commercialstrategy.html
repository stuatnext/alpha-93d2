Pricing Rules & Shock Controls

The discipline that keeps the rate card credible in a stabilisation year.

Rate-card structure

  • Rebook: 2026 price for identical scope only, inside the window.
  • 2027 list: public anchor for new sales and upgrades.
  • Cost-led quote: required for bespoke activations, side events and takeovers.
  • Discounts need an approved commercial get, such as faster signature, bigger scope, renewal commitment or case study rights.

Shock bands

0% to 20%: standard annual increase. 21% to 40%: buyer-facing value rationale required. 40%+: repositioning and director approval. After a churn year driven by unjustified increases, every shock-band move must carry proof.

Proof-of-Value Programme

Price increases must carry proof. This is how the +€316K stays defensible.

Measure

  • Lead retrieval in every stand.
  • Session badge scanning.
  • Track sponsor scan data where contracted.
  • Curated-introduction outcome summary.
  • Footfall tracking where viable.

Amplify

  • Exhibitor profile pages.
  • Interactive floor-plan profiles.
  • AI-matched target-attendee lists.
  • Target-account briefing for curated-introduction sponsors.

Report

  • Post-event proof reports.
  • Delegate seniority mix.
  • Lead and engagement summary.
  • Meeting outcome summary for introduction package.
