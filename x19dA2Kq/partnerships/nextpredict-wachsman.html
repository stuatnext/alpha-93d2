What NEXTPredict is

The room

NEXTPredict is a senior, category-forming event for prediction markets and event contracts. The event is built for the people operating, funding, regulating, analysing and supplying the market.

The subject

Prediction markets now touch finance, fintech, Web3, sports, media, compliance, market data, trading infrastructure, legal classification and consumer trust.

The format

No exhibition floor this year. The event is led by content, networking, curated meetings, brand positioning and senior discussion across three stages.

The opportunity

For the right Wachsman clients, this can be a speaking opportunity, sponsorship opportunity, relationship-building platform or category-positioning moment.

Why this matters now

Prediction markets are moving faster than the language around them. The category is no longer one clean lane. It is splitting across consumer distribution, institutional infrastructure, compliance, data, settlement, sports integrity, market making, media, regulation and public perception.

FinanceEvent contracts are entering public-market analysis, brokerage strategy and institutional workflow conversations.
Web3Crypto wallets, on-chain markets, token communities and settlement infrastructure remain central to parts of the category.
SportSports may be the fastest adoption route, but it also raises serious questions around integrity, data rights and consumer perception.
TrustThe market is short of trust architecture: surveillance, compliance, identity, resolution, data quality and responsible scaling.

Two routes for Wachsman clients

Speaking

For senior people with a real point of view

We are looking for C-level executives, founders, senior decision makers and recognised category experts who can move the conversation forward.

  • Founder, CEO, C-suite or key commercial decision maker
  • Direct relevance to prediction markets, event contracts or adjacent infrastructure
  • Clear expertise across regulation, market structure, data, compliance, media, Web3, finance, sport or consumer product
  • All proposed speakers are reviewed by the NEXTPredict conference content team
Sponsorship

For companies that want visibility in the room

Sponsorship is best suited to companies that want strategic positioning, senior meetings, brand visibility and direct access to an emerging category while it is still being shaped.

  • Market infrastructure, data, identity, payments, risk or compliance providers
  • Web3, fintech, trading, brokerage or exchange-adjacent companies
  • Sports integrity, official data, media intelligence or research firms
  • Clients with a clear commercial reason to be seen by prediction-market operators, investors and regulators

The quality bar

NEXTPredict is being built carefully. The aim is to put the right people in the right rooms, not collect easy logos.

Seniority matters

Speaking roles are reserved for C-level executives, founders, senior decision makers or genuinely credible subject-matter experts.

Fit matters

Every client should connect to the event’s core themes: market structure, regulation, infrastructure, data, compliance, media, sports or Web3.

Substance matters

Speaking opportunities are curated carefully. The strongest fit is usually a C-level leader, founder or senior decision maker who can add genuine value to the programme and speak to the category questions NEXTPredict is being built around.

Speaking rule: all sessions, including panels, firesides and workshops, are developed with the NEXTPredict conference content team. The team will review speaker relevance, session balance and programme fit before anything is confirmed.
