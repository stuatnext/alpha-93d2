Forward-looking planning timeline

2028 Commercial Readiness Timeline

A target-date plan for when each 2028 project should complete each stage of commercial readiness, with enough runway for Q3/Q4 2027 sales conversations before buyer marketing budgets are locked.

Planning year: 2028 Budget-cycle rule: Q3/Q4 2027 Owners: Project setup + Commercial outputs Applies to events, media, communities and marketing-led projects

Purpose

This document sets the optimal target dates for having each commercial stage complete for 2028 projects.

It is the forward-looking planning spine: when evidence needs to be collected, when commercial strategy should be approved, when GTM should be ready, and when Sales, Marketing, CRM and collateral should be prepared.

The commercial reason for this timing is simple: many sponsors, advertisers and strategic partners negotiate, defend or lock their marketing budgets in Q3 and Q4. If Sales does not have approved products, pricing and a credible narrative during that window, we are late before the event year even begins.

Planning assumption

This plan assumes the 2028 portfolio follows the same broad cadence as 2027: New York in March, Valletta in May, NEXTPredict in October, Retreats in October and November, and Media / Community products running year-round.

If actual event dates move, the relative rule should remain: commercial readiness must be complete before Sales and Marketing are expected to scale.

For events and higher-value sponsorship products, early sales discussions should begin in Q3 or Q4 2027 wherever possible.

1. What “ready” means

For this timeline, “ready” does not mean every final campaign asset is live. It means the project has the approved foundation needed for proper sales, marketing and delivery execution.

0. Planning route

Project type, owner, planning level, approval route and inputs confirmed.

1. Evidence pack

Sales, product, audience, delivery, financial, competitor and client evidence collected.

2. Commercial strategy

Products, pricing, packages, targets, margin logic, inventory and rules approved.

3. GTM framework

Positioning, buyers, channels, sales direction, marketing direction and handoffs clear.

4. Sales strategy

Account plan, pipeline targets, renewal plan, objection handling and rep focus ready.

5. Marketing strategy

Campaign direction, audience plan, channel mix, content needs, landing pages and conversion goals ready.

6. Setup complete

CRM, reporting, collateral brief, delivery workflow, proof plan and launch dependencies ready.

7. Sales window

Sales can enter buyer budget conversations without inventing pricing, products or promises.

4. Budget-cycle logic

Why Q3/Q4 2027 matters

For many sponsors, advertisers and strategic partners, marketing budgets are negotiated, protected or locked during Q3 and Q4. If Sales does not have approved products, pricing, packages and a credible narrative during that window, the project risks missing the buyer's budget cycle.

Risk What happens How this timeline prevents it
Sales starts after budgets are locked Buyers may like the event or product but have no remaining budget, or they push spend into the next planning cycle. Commercial strategy, GTM and early sales direction are ready before or during Q3/Q4 budget planning.
Sales has no approved pricing Commercial conversations become vague, discount-led or dependent on follow-up promises. Product and pricing approval happens before strategic outreach scales.
Collateral is late Sales cannot make a confident case while buyers are comparing annual partnership options. Minimum viable collateral is ready before budget conversations peak.
Marketing starts too late The market has no proof, narrative or urgency when Sales needs support. Marketing strategy follows GTM early enough to support sales conversations with authority, proof and positioning.

5. Event planning notes

NEXT Summit New York 2028

Target ready date: 31 August 2027.

New York happens in March, so an August readiness point gives Sales and Marketing roughly six months of clean runway. Soft rebooking should start immediately after the 2027 event. Formal rebooking should begin once pricing and products are approved. Early sales conversations need to be active in Q3 2027 because sponsors will already be shaping 2028 budget allocations.

NEXT Summit Valletta 2028

Target ready date: 30 September 2027.

Valletta needs the 2027 post-event evidence pack: what sold, what churned, product performance, sponsor proof, ticket performance, delivery issues and content learnings. The commercial model should be ready by end July 2027 so GTM, Sales and Marketing can be ready by September. This allows Sales to enter Q3/Q4 buyer budget conversations with credible 2028 packages.

NEXTPredict 2028

Target ready date: 31 March 2028.

The full strategy should use 2027 performance evidence, but category-shaping sponsor conversations should begin in Q4 2027 wherever possible. Large buyers may already be allocating 2028 marketing and partnership budgets before the full 2028 GTM is complete.

Retreats 2028

Target ready date: 30 April 2028.

Retreats are premium relationship products. The buyer profile, pricing, experience design, delivery capacity and senior outreach plan need to be ready by April. Early interest conversations should still begin in Q4 2027 where possible, especially with strategic partners planning annual budgets.

10. Risk if targets are missed

Missed milestone Likely consequence Corrective action
Q3/Q4 sales discussion window missed Buyers may have already committed or protected their 2028 marketing budgets elsewhere. Prioritise minimum viable sales materials: product logic, pricing guardrails, proof points and senior outreach list.
Evidence pack late Commercial strategy gets built on anecdote and assumptions. Proceed with named assumptions only where risk is acceptable. Escalate missing critical inputs.
Commercial strategy late GTM, Sales, Marketing, CRM and collateral all compress. Prioritise pricing, products, packages and financial assumptions first. Everything else follows.
GTM late Sales and Marketing create their own interpretation of the market, which creates drift. Issue a short GTM mandate first, then expand into full GTM where needed.
Sales strategy late Pipeline generation starts too late or without clear target accounts and priorities. Prioritise named accounts, monthly targets, package focus and rebooking / renewal logic.
Marketing strategy late Campaigns become asset production rather than market-making. Prioritise audience, message, channel, landing page, tracking and conversion plan.
Collateral late Sales improvises, proposals become inconsistent and pricing discipline weakens. Ship minimum viable collateral first: one-pager, rate card, proposal template and objection bank.
CRM late Reporting becomes fiction within weeks. Set minimum tracking fields before launch: source, product, buyer type, status, price, discount and owner.

11. Meeting rule

  • Kickoff meetings should be broader project readiness meetings, not Commercial-only meetings.
  • Project / Product Owners set up the meetings and coordinate attendance.
  • Commercial owns the commercial model, GTM framework and commercial recommendations.
  • Sales discussions for major 2028 projects should begin in Q3/Q4 2027 where possible because buyer budgets are being planned or locked then.
  • Brainstorming should happen asynchronously before approval meetings.
  • Ideas should be submitted as recommendations with evidence, impact and owner where possible.
  • Approval meetings should approve, reject or assign.
  • Every missed deadline gets an owner, corrective action and revised date.
  • Every meeting ends with a decision log update.
