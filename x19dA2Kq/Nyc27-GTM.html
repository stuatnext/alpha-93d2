01 · Mission and positioning

The commercial decision is made

NYC 2027 is a lift-and-shift of the proven 2026 product set, revalued at a Manhattan premium. The team should stop re-debating pricing and start selling against the approved rate card, the deal desk and the scarcity story.

The model

Scarcity, not square metres

European competitors scale to 30,000+ delegates and commoditise floor space. We sell a capped premium room: 20-stand max floor, max 2 stands per brand, multi-day format engineered around a c.1,500 peak-capacity venue environment.

The proof

51.4% executive density

Verified 2026 registration data: 30.4% Founders/MDs plus 21% C-Suite. Operators 28.6%, suppliers 22.6%. This concentration of decision-makers is what justifies premium pricing against mass-market events.

Strategic continuity

Lift and shift, repriced

The 2027 physical product set mirrors 2026 to protect operational bandwidth for the NEXTPredict launch. The valuation does not: historically underpriced inventory is rebalanced to reflect genuine scarcity, buyer density and New York delivery value.

What it is not

Not a discount-led floor

Not a volume expo, not a CPM branding play, not a stand bazaar. No steep discounting culture: 15% hard ceiling, 5% on power inventory, sweeteners instead of price erosion.

Mission line: We are selling the densest executive room in the industry's North American calendar — and pricing it like we believe it.

Value proposition

Every buyer type gets a different reason to pay the premium. Sales conversations lead with outcomes, not inventory lists.

Headline / Diamond partners

Pipeline, not branding

Executive Matchmaking turns partnership into ABM: 10 curated double opt-in introductions at Headline (€280k), up to 5 at Diamond (€135k+). Below €135k receives zero included introductions. This line is non-negotiable.

Exhibitors

A quality-filtered floor

The €27k floor for a 3x2 stand is a quality filter that biases the room toward well-funded corporate peers. Scarce large stands (€85k–€110k) are priced on placement and exclusivity, not volume discounts.

Content partners

Authority on three stages

Branded sessions, custom sessions, presenter slots and paid non-branded panels across Leadership, Stage 2 and Stage 3 — €1.125M of protected content inventory. Non-branded panels are paid product, never sweeteners.

Attendees

The room is the product

Full Event is the revenue engine; VIP is a materially elevated hospitality tier (true VIP lounge, premium F&B, hosted networking) — not a lanyard upgrade. Conference Only exists to anchor the premium baseline.
