02 · Bitwise cheat sheet

Who they are and why they matter

Use this as the two-minute briefing before the call. The goal is to understand why Bitwise has a credible strategic reason to care about NEXTPredict.

What Bitwise is

Crypto asset manager

Bitwise is a global crypto asset manager with around $11bn in client assets, over 70 investment products and a product set spanning ETFs, SMAs, private funds, hedge strategies and staking.

Who they sell to

Wealth and institutional channels

They serve private wealth teams, RIAs, family offices, institutional investors, banks and broker-dealers. This means their buyer universe overlaps with serious finance, not retail hype.

Why Matt matters

ETF credibility

Matt Hougan is Bitwise CIO. He was previously CEO of ETF.com, CEO at Inside ETFs and co-authored the CFA Institute monograph on ETFs.

Why now

Prediction-market ETFs

Bitwise has been reported as one of the asset managers filing for ETFs tied to prediction-market outcomes. That gives them a live reason to educate the market.

How they relate to NEXTPredict

The bridge from crypto ETFs to event-contract ETFs

  • They understand how to package a new asset class for mainstream investors.
  • They have ETF credibility, which gives the prediction-market ETF topic weight.
  • They need education, media credibility and institutional framing around a new product category.
  • NEXTPredict gives them a room with platforms, operators, investors, legal, compliance, media and infrastructure stakeholders.
The commercial interpretation

This is not a crypto sponsor lead

  • Do not frame Bitwise as a crypto logo looking for a speaking slot.
  • Frame them as an asset manager trying to define the institutional story around prediction-market ETFs.
  • The sell is education, authority, media visibility and selected relationship-building.
  • The strongest package should connect stage presence with media or curated stakeholder access.

06 · Red lines and gets

Protect pricing after the sold slot

The temptation will be to “make room” for Matt. Do not do that without value coming back.

Do not agree

On the call, avoid these

  • No free Leadership Stage exception.
  • No “prediction-market ETF partner” title without paid commitment.
  • No Media Lounge access unless purchased.
  • No private meeting support without a paid product attached.
  • No stage promise without Rory/content sign-off.
  • No VIP access as a casual sweetener.
If they resist price

Ask for value back

  • Matt confirmed in writing.
  • Pre-event Q&A or quote.
  • Bitwise promotion to their audience.
  • Post-event amplification.
  • Fast signature and payment.
  • Case study / sponsor success permission.
  • 2027 renewal or first-refusal conversation.
  • Introductions to relevant ETF or institutional contacts.
