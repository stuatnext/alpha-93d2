# ChatGPT image-generation prompts — NEXT Retreats 2026 banners

Use these to produce banner creative in ChatGPT's image generator. **Attach a photo
from a previous NEXT Retreat in the same message** — the prompts use it as a
colour-graded background, which looks far better (and more reliable) than a generated
scene.

CTA across the set is **"Become a sponsor"** (short: "Sponsor") — the campaign is
selling paid sponsorships, so we never say "apply" or "partnership" (both get misread
as free).

**Tips**
- Match the crop to the shape: landscape photos for Leaderboard/Megabanner, a portrait
  shot for the Skyscraper.
- Image models render small text imperfectly. For the thin wide formats, if the text
  is garbled, append: *"generate the graded background + logo only, leave clear space
  for text"* and set the wording in Canva/Figma over the export.
- Attach the real logo (`brand/next-logo.png`) and add *"use this exact logo, do not
  redraw it"* for brand accuracy.
- Models can't hit exact pixel dims — generate at the stated aspect ratio, then
  resize/crop to spec.
