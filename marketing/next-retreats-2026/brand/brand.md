# NEXT Retreats — brand quick reference

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Canvas black | `#080808` | Primary background |
| Signature yellow | `#FFCF33` | Accent, CTAs, chevron, highlights |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Wordmark, headlines |
| Muted grey | `#B0B0B0` | Secondary / supporting text |
| Europe accent A | `#0F204C` | Mediterranean blue (glow) |
| Europe accent B | `#2BB7B3` | Aegean teal (place name) |
| LatAm accent A | `#7A1854` | Deep magenta (glow) |
| LatAm accent B | `#FF8A4C` | Caribbean sunset (place name) |

**Logo:** `next-logo.png` — white `NEXT` wordmark + yellow `>` chevron + yellow `.io`.
Built for dark backgrounds; do not place on white without a dark container.

**Type:** Big Shoulders (condensed) for display headlines; Outfit (geometric sans,
matches the wordmark) for sub-heads and CTAs; Work Sans for body.

**Voice:** confident, exclusive, understated. "By invitation only." Never shouty.
The scarcity does the selling — 50 + 50, closed-door, no media.
