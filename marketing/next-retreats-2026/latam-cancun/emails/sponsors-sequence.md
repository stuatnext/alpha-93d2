# Email campaign — NEXT Retreat LatAm 2026 · Sponsorship

**Audience:** Suppliers / platforms / vendors who want face-time with senior Latin
American operators.
**Goal (lead gen):** book sponsorship conversations → *Become a sponsor* enquiries.
**Cadence:** 3-touch over ~2 weeks; stop on reply/booking.
**CTA link:** https://next.io/next-retreats/latam-cancun/ → *Become a sponsor*

> **Naming note:** this is a paid sponsorship sell. Lead with the word
> **"sponsorship"** (not "partnership" — it gets misread as free) and don't say
> "apply." A visible price anchor in Email 2 removes any doubt that it's a
> commercial opportunity.

**Sponsorship packages (reference):** Headline €85k (×1 — speaking slot, 4 passes),
General €35k (×20 — 2 passes), Activity €10–20k (×5).
